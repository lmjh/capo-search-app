Capo Search

What is Capo Search?

Capo Search is an app to help guitarists quickly find easier ways to play songs using a capo. Just enter the chords you want to play and Capo Search will automatically tell you where to put your capo and how to play your selected chords using simple open chord shapes.

If any of that sounds confusing to you, check out the FAQ section for more information!

How do I use Capo Search?

Click the buttons to select the chords of the song you want to play.

The search results update automatically. Find a set of results you like and put your capo on the indicated fret.

With your capo in place, use the fingering shown at the top of each box to play the chord marked at the bottom.

Frequently Asked Questions

A capo is a tool that guitarists use to change the pitch of the guitar’s strings. Putting a capo on the neck of the guitar lets a guitarist play a different set of chords using the chord shapes that they already know. Many beginners use a capo to play songs that would otherwise require learning to play more technically challenging chords.

Capo Search automatically disables the buttons for chords that aren’t compatible with the chords you’ve already selected. Unfortunately, not every combination of chords can be easily played using a capo and open chord shapes.

There are thousands of possible chord constructions, so to keep things simple Capo Search is limited to just Major and Minor chords. If the song you want to play uses different chords, like 7th chords, diminished chords, etc, you can try selecting the major chord of the variant you need. Once you’ve found a capo position, look up how to play the variant you need of the open chord shape that Capo Search suggests.

Capo Search is limited to the nine simplest open chords that most beginner guitarists learn in order to keep things as simple and streamlined as possible for beginners. There are potentially hundreds or thousands of ‘open chords’, so including every possibility would result in a very complicated app!
